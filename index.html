About

I'm a final-year Chemical Engineering student at Institut Teknologi Bandung, specializing in General Chemical Technology. My work sits at the intersection of process design and materials chemistry — recent thesis projects have taken me from direct air capture materials to nuclear process design.

Outside coursework, I've spent time on the plant floor as a process engineering intern, and taken on organizing roles within ITB's chemical engineering community. I like problems that force a model to hold up against reality — where an Aspen HYSYS simulation has to check out against an actual mass balance.
